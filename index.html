Enjoy up to $15,000 on a 2023 F-150 Lightning XLT Extended Range with $7,500 in Retail Bonus Cash,* plus $7,500 in potential federal tax credit for eligible buyers (not all customers qualify).**

*Program offer dates: 2/20/24-4/2/24. $7,500 Retail Bonus Cash on 2023 F-150 Lightning XLT Extended Range (Program #11170). Nontransferable out of household. Limit one per household. U.S. residents. Prior purchases not eligible. Take new retail delivery from an authorized Ford Dealer’s stock by 4/2/24. See dealer for details.

**Please consult with your own tax or legal professional to determine your individual eligibility. Tax credit amount is based on vehicle-specific component sourcing and assembly and may be subject to change. You may not claim the credit if (1) your adjusted gross income exceeds certain thresholds or (2) the MSRP of the vehicle, as configured, exceeds $80,000 (including all optional equipment attached to the vehicle at the time of delivery to the dealer, but excluding destination charges, optional items added by the dealer, taxes and fees). Tax credit may be applied at point of sale, subject to dealer availability, or as a potential future tax savings. The amount of your tax savings will depend on your individual tax circumstances. Incentives and additional rebates are not within Ford’s control. This information does not constitute tax or legal advice. For additional information, go to https://www.afdc.energy.gov/laws/409.
